Rierastudioart.com

This is the first website I ever fully developed (so it took around 3x as long as it should have). June 2016, I travelled to Havana, Cuba to work with a local artist, Samuel Riera and his assistant, Derbis Campos. I worked with them everyday to design and code a website to showcase the work they promote to the world outside of Cuba. I developed a custom php framework to handle including partials on every page, and also to generate all of the pages for the artists and exhibitions. I'm still working on making it mobile (I've learned my mobile-first lesson), but view it on a desktop by following this link: Rierastudioart.com
